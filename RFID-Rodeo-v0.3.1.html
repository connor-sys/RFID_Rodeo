Authorized use only

Authorized use. This is an offline reference and calculator. It transforms values you enter; it does not read, write, transmit, or clone any physical card by itself. Use it only within an expressly authorized and documented testing scope. Accessing, duplicating, or using access-control credentials without authorization may violate law, contract, or organizational policy — authorization can come from ownership or from explicit permission to test a credential owned by someone else, such as your employer.

Accuracy and production safety. Bit layouts and parity follow public specifications and, where a format’s exact rule isn’t published, the standard leading-even / trailing-odd Wiegand convention. Some formats are approximations. Outputs may be incomplete or incorrect. Do not use calculated values for production access decisions, credential issuance, or system changes without independently verifying them against the relevant specification and real hardware.

Each format shows its bit-position layout (from published field tables) plus a built-in calculator. Field widths and bit positions follow public specifications; where a format's exact parity mask is not published, parity uses the standard leading-even / trailing-odd convention. Undocumented bits are shown in grey. Formats marked Verified have their field positions and parity checked against the Proxmark3 client reference (RFID Research Group / Iceman fork); Documented formats follow published layouts with the standard parity convention; Reference formats are shown for completeness and are not computed. Bit positions are numbered from 1 at the leftmost (most-significant) bit of the frame, and fields are packed most-significant-bit first. Verify against a physical reader before production use.
